Grilled Fish Fillet

This grilled fish fillet recipe is simple, healthy, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 fish fillets (such as cod or tilapia)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat the grill or oven to medium-high heat (about 375°F or 190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the fish fillets with the oil and seasoning mixture on both sides.
  4. Place the fillets on the grill or in the oven and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Remove the fillets from the heat and let them rest for a couple of minutes.
  6. Sprinkle with chopped parsley before serving, if desired.
  7. Serve the grilled fish fillets with your favorite side dish, such as rice or a salad.
